Privacy Policy

Effective date: 2 July 2026 · Applies to the iOS app "Tıp Dili" (bundle id com.tipdili.app) and its home-screen widget.

Summary. Tıp Dili does not collect, transmit, sell, or share any personal data. There are no accounts, no analytics, no advertising, no tracking, and no third-party SDKs. Everything you do in the app stays on your device. The only network activity is performed by Apple's own App Store frameworks when you view prices for, purchase, or restore the optional "Tıp Dili Pro" subscription — that payment relationship is between you and Apple.

1. Who is responsible ("data controller")

The app is published by Mert Kaya Özkan (the "developer", "we"). For anything in this policy you can reach us at iletisim@alandili.com. Because the app is designed not to process personal data on our side, in practice there is no personal data held by us to access, correct, export, or erase — see Section 9.

2. Data we collect

None. Specifically, the app has:

  • no user accounts, registration, or login;
  • no backend server operated by us — nothing is uploaded anywhere;
  • no analytics, crash-reporting, advertising, or tracking technologies;
  • no third-party SDKs of any kind;
  • no cookies or similar identifiers (the app contains no web content);
  • no access to your contacts, photos, location, microphone, camera, or health data.

3. Data stored only on your device

To function, the app keeps a small set of preferences in Apple's standard on-device storage (the app sandbox). This data is created by you, never leaves your device, and is not accessible to us:

  • the optional display name you may enter during onboarding (used only to greet you inside the app; you may leave it blank);
  • your chosen level (beginner / intermediate / advanced), content language, learning goal, and medical-specialty interests;
  • terms you mark as favorites and your saved collections;
  • your daily progress (which terms you have seen) and streak counter;
  • reminder settings and onboarding state.

The app and its widget share these preferences through an Apple "App Group" container that also lives entirely on your device. Deleting the app permanently deletes all of this data. iOS device backups you configure (iCloud/computer) are governed by Apple, not by us.

4. In-app purchases and subscriptions (Apple)

Tıp Dili offers an optional auto-renewing subscription, "Tıp Dili Pro" (monthly or yearly). All purchases are processed entirely by Apple through your Apple ID:

  • The app uses Apple's StoreKit framework to display current prices and to check, on your device, whether your subscription is active.
  • We never receive your name, address, e-mail, or any payment details. We operate no purchase server and hold no purchase database.
  • Billing, renewal, cancellation, and refunds are handled by Apple under Apple's Media Services terms and Apple's Privacy Policy.

Communication between your device and the App Store for these purposes is the app's only network activity. It is initiated and secured by iOS.

5. Notifications

If you enable the daily reminder, notifications are scheduled locally on your device by iOS. There is no push server; no message travels over the internet. You can disable reminders anytime in the app's Profile screen or in iOS Settings → Notifications → Tıp Dili.

6. Content & offline operation

All medical terms, definitions, and examples are bundled inside the app. Reading, studying, quizzes, practice, favorites, and the widget all work fully offline.

7. E-mail correspondence

If you contact us at the address above, we will process the personal data you include (your e-mail address and message) solely to answer you. Legal basis: our legitimate interest in responding to inquiries (GDPR Art. 6(1)(f)) / communication initiated by you (KVKK Art. 5). Correspondence is kept only as long as needed to handle your request and is then deleted upon request or when no longer necessary. It is never used for marketing and never shared.

8. Children

The app is an educational dictionary suitable for general audiences and collects no data from anyone, including children. We do not knowingly process children's personal data. Purchases are controlled by Apple's parental controls (Screen Time / Ask to Buy).

9. Your rights (GDPR / KVKK)

Under the EU General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) and Türkiye's Personal Data Protection Law No. 6698 (KVKK), you have rights to access, rectify, erase, restrict, object to, and port personal data, and to lodge a complaint with a supervisory authority (in Türkiye: the Personal Data Protection Authority, KVKK; in the EU/EEA: your local DPA). Because we hold no personal data about app users, these rights are in practice satisfied by design; for on-device data you can erase everything by deleting the app. For e-mail correspondence (Section 7), write to us and we will honor your request without undue delay. We do not transfer data internationally and do not engage in automated decision-making or profiling.

10. Security

Your preferences live inside the iOS app sandbox and are protected by your device's security (passcode/Face ID, encryption at rest as provided by iOS). Since nothing is transmitted to us, there is no server-side data to breach.

11. Changes to this policy

If we change this policy (for example, if a future version of the app adds an online feature), the updated version will be posted on this page with a new effective date before the change takes effect in the app.

12. Contact

Privacy questions or requests: iletisim@alandili.com

Tıp Dili is for educational purposes only. It does not provide diagnosis, treatment, dosage, or medical advice, and it is not a medical device. Always consult a qualified healthcare professional for clinical decisions.

Uygulama ile widget bu tercihleri, yine tamamen cihazınızda kalan bir Apple "App Group" kapsayıcısı üzerinden paylaşır. Uygulamayı silmek bu verilerin tamamını kalıcı olarak siler. Yapılandırdığınız iOS yedeklemeleri (iCloud/bilgisayar) Apple'ın koşullarına tabidir, bize değil.
